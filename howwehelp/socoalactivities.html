Social & Community Activities

Creating joyful moments and meaningful connections for our elders.

Our Programs

Wesak Day Programs

“At Sahanodaya Elder Care Home, Wesak Day is celebrated with great reverence and joy. Residents participate in lighting traditional oil lamps, decorating the premises with colorful lanterns, and engaging in meditation and chanting sessions. Special sermons and storytelling sessions are organized to reflect on the life and teachings of Lord Buddha. The celebration fosters spiritual reflection, peace, and a sense of unity among the elders, allowing them to connect with their cultural and religious heritage.”

Monthly Full Moon Poya Days Programs

“Full Moon Poya Days are observed with religious activities, meditation sessions, and group prayers. Residents visit nearby temples or have in-house Poya Day ceremonies conducted by monks. Discussions on moral values and life lessons are arranged, encouraging mindfulness and introspection. These activities promote spiritual well-being, reinforce a calm and reflective environment, and strengthen communal bonds among the elders.”

Learn More

New Year Day Programs

“New Year celebrations at the care home are filled with cultural traditions and entertainment. Residents participate in traditional rituals such as lighting the hearth, offering blessings, and observing customs to welcome the year. Fun activities and games, cultural performances, music, and dance are arranged to create a joyful and lively atmosphere. Family-style meals and social interactions help foster happiness, togetherness, and engagement among residents.”

Learn More

Elders Day Programs

“Elders’ Day is a special occasion to honor and celebrate the contributions of senior residents. The care home organizes award ceremonies, cultural programs, and interactive sessions where elders share experiences and stories. Social activities, games, and group discussions create engagement and joy, ensuring that every resident feels valued and respected. This day strengthens the sense of appreciation, community, and belonging among all elders.”

Learn More

Welfare Programs

“Sahanodaya Elder Care Home organizes welfare programs aimed at enhancing the physical, emotional, and social well-being of its residents. These include health check-ups, exercise and yoga sessions, counseling, recreational activities, educational workshops, and skill-building programs. The goal is to empower elders, maintain their independence, and provide support for a comfortable and meaningful life within the community.”
