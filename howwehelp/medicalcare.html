How we care — Our medical approach

At Sunrise Elder Care we combine compassionate daily care with professional medical services. Our goal is to preserve dignity, improve wellbeing, and respond quickly when healthcare needs arise.

  • •
    Regular health assessments
    Routine vitals check, chronic condition monitoring and medication reviews.
  • •
    Medication management
    Secure storage, admin schedule and nurse oversight to avoid errors.
  • •
    Physiotherapy & mobility
    Daily exercises and therapy sessions designed to preserve mobility and reduce falls.
  • •
    Emergency response
    Trained staff, first-aid kits and transfer arrangements to hospitals when needed.

Services we provide

Practical, medical and supportive services offered daily.

Health Checkups

Daily monitoring and scheduled medical reviews to manage chronic conditions.

Medication Support

Correct dosing, documentation and nurse administration as needed.

Physiotherapy

Guided exercises, balance training and gentle rehabilitation programs.

Wound Care

Professional dressing changes and infection prevention routines.

Telemedicine

Remote specialist consultations when on-site expertise is not available.

Emergency Care

Rapid assessment and immediate first-aid with hospital transfer plans.
