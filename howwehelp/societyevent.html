Society & Club Events

School clubs and societies supporting our elders with kindness.

Our Society Contributions

Painting by School Clubs

Students help repaint walls, fences, and rooms to make the home brighter and more welcoming.

Cleaning Programs

Clubs organize cleaning days to tidy gardens, sweep floors, wash windows, and maintain the environment.

Repair & Maintenance

Volunteers fix broken furniture, repair pathways, and support small renovation tasks.

Decorations & Festive Setup

Societies decorate halls and gardens for Vesak, Christmas, New Year, and special events.

Tree Planting Events

Environmental clubs plant trees and maintain small gardens around the home.

Cultural Support Programs

School clubs perform dramas, dance, and music to bring joy to elders.
