Donate

Help us provide meals, medical supplies and comfort items to our elders. Schedule a drop-off or donate funds.

How to donate

You can donate money, fresh or packaged food, or household & care items. Use the form to schedule a drop-off or record a monetary gift. For large donations or pickups, please contact us at +94 71 123 4567.

Accepted Food Items

  • • Packaged dry foods (rice, lentils, flour)
  • • Canned foods, instant mixes, powdered milk
  • • Fresh vegetables & fruits (in good condition)
  • • Cooked meals (only when arranged in advance)

Accepted Non-food Items

  • • Bedding, towels (clean)
  • • Incontinence supplies, adult diapers (unused)
  • • Mobility aids (walkers, canes) — check first
  • • Toiletries & hygiene supplies

Note: Please label donations if they are specific (e.g., “for Mr. Silva”). Avoid perishable items without prior notice.
